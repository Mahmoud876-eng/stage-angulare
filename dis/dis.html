Late Invoice Payment Dispute Management

Definition

A payment dispute occurs when a client fails to pay an invoice on time or contests the amount...

Main Goals

  • Identify the cause of the delay
  • Maintain a positive client relationship
  • Recover the unpaid amount
  • Avoid legal actions

Standard Dispute Handling Process

  1. Detect the Delay – Use ERP or billing systems, with alerts
  2. Send Friendly Reminders – Email or phone call
  3. Analyze the Dispute – Check facts, contact client
  4. Resolve Amicably – Plan or invoice correction
  5. Follow-up & Close – Track until full resolution

Sample Reminder Email

Subject: Follow-up on Unpaid Invoice #4523

Dear [Client Name],

According to our records, invoice #4523 for €820, due on June 5, 2025, remains unpaid.

Please let us know if there’s any issue regarding this payment or kindly proceed with the settlement.

Best regards,
Accounting Department

Best Practices

  • Document communications
  • Save all invoice records
  • Set clear Terms & Conditions
  • Assess client risk
